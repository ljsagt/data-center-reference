Watchlist

  • Rack-level liquid cooling stack: Delta and TPU-related evidence point to cold plates, manifolds, CDUs, and L11 assembly as a more visible bottleneck cluster than the current table captures.
  • 800V HVDC / solid-state transformer: Important architecture change, but only partly captured by current power semiconductor and transformer rows.
  • Behind-the-meter power: Important system constraint, but still too broad to assign to one engine, switchgear, or fuel-system component.
