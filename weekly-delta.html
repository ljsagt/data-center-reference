Watchlist

  • 800V HVDC equipment: SST, SSCB, BBU/CBU, and power-shelf architecture need cleaner component rows.
  • DC fiber: GPU-generation replacement cycles could create recurring demand, but no fiber row exists.
  • PCIe switches and retimers: Switches look stronger in supernode designs; retimers may peak with orthogonal backplanes.
  • EMIB-T and package alternatives: Important for inference ASICs, but still mapped only indirectly through advanced packaging.
  • PCB substitution: PTFE, ceramic, glass, thin-film circuits, all-optical networks, and CPO could alter PCB intensity after Rubin.
  • Custom ASIC and CPU alternatives: Qualcomm, Google TPU, AWS Trainium, MediaTek, ARM, and DPU architecture matter, but not as component rows.
